The Behind-The-Scenes Story of Byeon Bread

A good friend of mine, James Magahern, had an idea that the word byeond rhymes with my last name Byeon. There is a local sandwich store / bakery called Beyond Bread based in Tucson, Arizona. Himself, his twin brother Charles, me and other gang members used to go there for lunch together.

One day, he took a picture of a brown paper bag for takeout from Byeond Bread to make this Byeon Bread illustration, and he posted it on Facebook. Everyone loved the idea and agreed it just looks like me, although I slightly disagree. Well, I had very short hair and was wearing rectangular glasses at that time, so I could see where it is coming from. Ever since then, I've been using this as an unofficial portrait of mine.
